Simple 3D AR Model Viewer Template

This page demonstrates a single, embeddable 3D AR model viewer. This shows how the pop up 3d view window will look and function.

Dimensions: W 90cm x H 160cm x D 20cm

×

Scan this QR code with your phone to open the webpage. Then, tap the AR button on the 3D model to activate Augmented Reality.

Once in AR: Slowly move your phone to scan the floor/surface, then tap the screen to place the model. Rotate it with two fingers.
